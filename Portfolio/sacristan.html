Our Lady of Lourdes Altar Server Ministry
The altar server ministry at Our Lady of Lourdes Parish offers a meaningful way for young parishioners to serve God and the community during the Holy Mass. Altar servers assist priests and deacons with important tasks such as carrying the processional cross, holding the Roman Missal, and preparing the altar for the Eucharist. Through these responsibilities, servers help foster a spirit of reverence and ensure that liturgical celebrations are conducted with dignity and grace.

Inspired by the humility and devotion of Our Lady of Lourdes, this ministry welcomes boys and girls who have received their First Holy Communion, typically beginning at age 10. No prior experience is requiredâ€”just a sincere desire to serve. Comprehensive training is provided to help new altar servers feel confident and prepared for their role in the parish.

Serving as an altar server is a rewarding experience that encourages spiritual growth, builds community, and deepens faith. By participating in this ministry, young people have the opportunity to grow closer to Christ and contribute to the life of the parish. For more information or to join, please contact the parish office or the Altar Server Coordinator.
